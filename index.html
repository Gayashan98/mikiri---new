Why R.K. Dhanesh Mee Kiri?

We hold our craftsmanship to the highest standards, preserving flavor, purity, and Sri Lankan heritage in every single clay pot.

🌾

100% Pure Buffalo Milk

Sourced fresh daily from healthy grass-fed local buffaloes grazing freely on rich organic pastures in deep Sri Lanka. Absolutely no water, milk powder, or preservatives added.

🏺

Traditional Clay Baking

Fermented slowly inside genuine porous clay pots (*hatti*). The clay absorbs excess whey, giving our curd a thick, creamy, velvety texture and unique earthy aroma.

🔥

Woodfire Milk Cooking

Boiled to perfection over traditional village woodfire stoves. This slow-cooking method caramelizes the natural sugars in the milk, giving our curd a rich, caramelized undertone.

The Purity Circle

How We Make Premium Curd

1

Fresh Organic Milking

Our day begins before sunrise. Premium, rich buffalo milk is collected from pure local pastures under strict hygiene standards.

2

Slow Fire Boiling

The milk is heated and stirred continuously inside vast clay vessels on open woodfire stoves to guarantee optimal pasteurization and natural caramelization.

3

Clay Fermentation

Natural starter culture is gently mixed into the cooled milk. It is then poured into sterile, traditional clay pots and left to set naturally in a humidity-controlled cooling cellar.

Natural Grass-Fed Buffaloes

Fresh Organic Dairy Sourced Directly from the Heart of Nature

Our pastures are situated in rural, untouched areas of Sri Lanka, offering a stress-free environment for buffalo herds. Natural feeding produces high-fat, high-calcium, exceptionally nutritious milk, the secret behind R.K. Dhanesh's distinctively thick curd texture.

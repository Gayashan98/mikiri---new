Frequently Asked Questions

Do you charge for home deliveries? ↓

Deliveries within the Hambantota town limit are completely free of charge! For long-distance orders (such as Colombo, Galle, or Gampaha districts), we charge a small logistics fee to dispatch them in thermal cooled packages.

Can I return the empty clay pots? ↓

Yes! In line with our eco-friendly organic promise, you can return dry, empty, clean R.K. Dhanesh clay pots to any of our outlets. We will give you a Rs. 20 refund or discount per pot for your next curd purchase!

How long does the curd stay fresh? ↓

Since our curd is 100% natural and preservative-free, it stays perfectly fresh for up to 7-10 days inside a standard home refrigerator. Keep it covered to prevent it from absorbing other refrigerator odors.
